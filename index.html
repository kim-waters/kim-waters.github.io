About Me

I am a bright and analytical graduate of the University of Exeter with a first class master’s degree in Physics with Astrophysics. I consistently perform highly across a range of fields, achieving first class marks in 25 out of 29 total modules at university.
I am naturally skilled and experienced in coding, achieving an A in A-level computing and first class marks in University coding modules. I developed my coding skills in my masters dissertation on computational modelling of exo-planetary atmospheres, and further still during a 12 week graduate cloud computing course with the IN4.0 Talent Academy. The latter instilled in me a commercial awareness and improved my professional conduct as I interned with industrial partners as a technology consultant. My enthusiasm for technology is self-driven, and since graduating I have taught myself HTML/CSS and become an AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner. I also foster my creative side, shown by my writing for music magazines and the campus newspaper throughout my time at university. I find that linking the two sides of my brain keeps me sharp and innovative.
